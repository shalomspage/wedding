OUR LOVE STORY

Our Story

#Janeva24

This epic love story officially began on March 6 th 2023, thanks to Emah a mutual friend, who thought it was nice to make the introduction. Our journey started gradually, and it took only a short time to realize that our goals were aligned and that things moved seamlessly. Despite challenges such as the distance between us and discouragement by a couple of persons who meant well, we kept pushing and kept faith. Long video calls and audio calls at any second, we got. Keeping each other constantly in our daily activities that sometimes we forget that there is any distance between us. By November 19 th 2023, I was convinced when Oreva asked me to walk this walk with him and said he sees no other woman as his wife but me. I said yes to him, and he promised to make it official by 2024. All he said was Baby, pick a date, and let’s work towards it, I did, and that was it. We got legally hitched on the 5 th of November, and we can’t wait to embark on this journey together and celebrate with you all on the 16 th of November, 2024. Cheers
